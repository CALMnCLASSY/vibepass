Frequently Asked Questions

How is hospitality different from a general ticket?

FIFA World Cup 2026™ hospitality packages are ticket-inclusive offerings and series that provide premium seating, exclusive entertainment, and upscale food and beverage, with service levels ranging from private suites to shared lounges – and other festive product options within the stadium's secure perimeter – along with a variety of additional one-of-a-kind experiences. These packages provide an elevated experience beyond the standard offerings of a general ticket at host venues.

Can I buy hospitality packages from other companies?

On Location is the only Official Hospitality Provider of the FIFA World Cup 2026™. Ticket-inclusive hospitality packages will be sold exclusively by On Location and its Officially Appointed Sales Agents (see list here).

Fans are urged to purchase hospitality packages through FIFA.com/hospitality. Hospitality packages and tickets sourced from unofficial sales channels may not be valid.

How do I purchase a private suite at FIFA World Cup 2026™?

To purchase a FIFA World Cup 2026™ private suite, select your desired match. If the match is located in the U.S., you will be directed to complete the purchase online with a credit card via our U.S. Checkout Store.

If the selected match is located in Canada or Mexico, you will request to book via an online form, after which you will be prompted to complete the transaction via our Canada or Mexico Checkout Store, respectively. (After submitting a booking request, you will receive notification within minutes via email to complete the transaction.) After you check out and complete your order, you will receive an order confirmation via email. Please note the transaction will take place in the currency of the country in which the requested match will take place.

Are Private Suites available for multi-match hospitality packages?

Yes, private suites are available for multi-match hospitality packages. The more matches selected, the lower the per-match price of the package. If you are interested in multi-match private suite packages, please either contact an officially appointed sales agent or register your interest and an On Location representative or an officially appointed sales agent will contact you.
